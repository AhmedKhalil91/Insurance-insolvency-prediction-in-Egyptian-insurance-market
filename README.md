# Dataset used in our paper

## Data Source

The collected data of this work are obtained from the items of the annual financial statements of Egyptian property liability insurance companies that are published in the annual reports of the Egyptian insurance companies issued by the Egyptian Financial Supervisory Authority. All of these reports are publicly available.
